Jungle BJJ Team

About Us

Jungle Brazilian Jiu Jitsu team is a program focused on providing free Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training to those that would otherwise not be able to access these services. We are the first and only completely free Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program in Australia. We also provide free accommodation to high level athletes giving them an opportunity to progress their career and imerse themselves in a safe, clean, productive competition focused environment. We are located on a pristine, large and beautiful property set amongst the oldest rain forest on earth. Athletes are offered the opportunity to train multiple times a day and progress rapidly in our sport.
